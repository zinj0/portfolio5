99 - Brazilian Rideshare Company (2019-2020)

Role: Art Director & Web Designer

Duration: 6 months

Overview:

As an Art Director at EYXO, I led the charge in planning and executing campaigns for 99, a leading Brazilian rideshare company. My role centered around delivering clear, yet culturally rich (“brazilianness”) communications for the app’s drivers, passengers, and the broader branding strategy. Our main challenge was to craft a message that maintained its distinct personality while being universally comprehensible.

Key Contributions:

  • Strategic Planning: Collaborated with my team to develop and execute a cohesive branding strategy that spanned digital and print media.
  • Visual Campaigns: Created impactful visuals that enhanced the brand’s identity and resonated with diverse audiences.
  • Email Marketing: Designed and implemented HTML and CSS email campaigns, significantly boosting engagement and conversion rates.

Achievements:

  • User Engagement: Successfully improved user interaction through aesthetically appealing and well-structured visual campaigns.
  • Brand Awareness: Elevated the brand’s presence across various platforms, leading to increased recognition and market share.

Skills Utilized:

  • Design & Development: Utilized Adobe Creative Suite and advanced web development skills to produce dynamic and responsive content.
  • UI/UX Design: Focused on user-friendly designs that improved overall user experience.
  • Cross-Functional Collaboration: Worked closely with various departments to ensure brand consistency and effective communication.

Key Takeaways:

  • Cultural Adaptation: Adapted design strategies to cater to the unique preferences of different cultural segments within the market.
  • Problem-Solving: Demonstrated strong analytical skills in identifying and overcoming design challenges.
  • Leadership & Communication: Led a creative team, fostering a collaborative environment and delivering high-quality outcomes.
